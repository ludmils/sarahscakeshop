Our story

Sarah’s is a locally-owned business that was established in 2003. What began as a small café & catering company in Wildwood, Missouri, turned into a specialized dessert destination. In 2010, Sarah’s made the move to Chesterfield, Missouri, and focused more on cakes, desserts, and cupcakes. At that time, we also added St. Louis’ first dessert truck to our family. While the sweeter side of the business continued to grow, we never lost our passion for our small café roots, so in 2016 we decided to bring our extensive dessert and updated café menus to historic Old Towne Eureka, Missouri. After years of customers requesting we ship our items, the decision was made to implement Nation Wide Shipping.  2023 is looking to be a year of changes at Sarah’s!

Nutritional Information

Please be advised that all food prepared in this facility may contain or may come in contact with known allergens, such as: eggs, dairy, peanuts and tree nuts, wheat, gluten, and soy.


We will take every precaution to prevent cross-contamination of all orders with a known allergy concern but cannot completely guarantee our products to be allergen-free, as we do have all of the above ingredients present in our facility.


If you have a severe allergy, please let us know, as we may not be able to accommodate your order.﻿
